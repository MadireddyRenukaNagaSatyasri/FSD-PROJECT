Discover the Soul of South India: Chennai

Masala Dosa

A thin, crispy fermented rice crepe filled with a savory potato mash. Served with fresh coconut chutney and hot sambar, it is the king of Chennai tiffins.

Idli Sambar

Soft, fluffy steamed rice cakes served with a flavorful lentil-based vegetable stew. This is considered one of the healthiest breakfasts in the world.

Filter Coffee

A strong, milky coffee brewed in a traditional metal filter and served frothy in a stainless steel 'Dabarah'. It is the aromatic heart of Chennai.

Chicken 65

A spicy, deep-fried chicken dish originating from the famous Buhari Hotel in Chennai. It is flavored with curry leaves, ginger, and garlic.

Medu Vada

Crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, these savory donut-shaped lentil fritters are best enjoyed when dipped in hot sambar.

Ambur Biryani

A unique style of biryani popular in Chennai, made with Seeraga Samba rice and tender meat, offering a distinct aroma and mild spice level.
