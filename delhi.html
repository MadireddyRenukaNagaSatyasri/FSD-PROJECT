Dilli Ka Swad: New Delhi Food Explorer

Chole Bhature

Spicy chickpeas served with fluffy, deep-fried bread. A legendary Delhi breakfast staple. Often served with spicy pickles and onions.

Butter Chicken

Creamy, tomato-based gravy with tender tandoori chicken. Invented in Delhi, this dish is a global favorite for its rich, velvety texture.

Parathas

Golden, crispy stuffed flatbreads. From the lanes of Gali Paranthe Wali to modern tables, these are the heart of Delhi's street food.

Gol Gappe

Crispy hollow spheres filled with spicy tangy water and potatoes. A burst of flavor in every bite that every Delhiite swears by.

Rajma Chawal

Comfort in a bowl: Thick kidney bean curry served over steaming basmati rice. The ultimate soul food for any North Indian.

Kebabs

Succulent, smoky meat grilled to perfection over charcoal. From Seekh to Shami, Old Delhi's kebabs are a royal treat for meat lovers.
